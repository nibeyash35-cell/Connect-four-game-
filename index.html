How to Play

  1. Choose a mode: Human vs AI or Human vs Human.
  2. Click any column to drop your disc into the board.
  3. Discs fall to the lowest open space in that column.
  4. Take turns and try to connect 4 discs before your opponent.
  5. The AI plays automatically when AI mode is on.

Rules

  • The board has 7 columns and 6 rows.
  • You cannot move a disc after placing it.
  • Winning lines can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • If the board fills completely with no winner, the game ends in a tie.

Features

  • Neon arcade-style theme.
  • AI opponent with difficulty levels.
  • Sound effects for drops and wins.
  • Score tracker for wins and ties.
  • Restart button for instant rematches.
